*
* Export CPU to x86_64 or aarch64to build for a specific architecture
*
* Unset CPU to build for all architectures
*
* To build ROS2 do the following:
*
*   1- Export the variables above
*   2- cd ros2_${ROS2DIST}/qnx_deps
*   3- ./build-deps.sh
*   4- cd ros2_${ROS2DIST}
*   5- ./colcon-ignore.sh
*   6- ./build-ros2.sh
